MINNESOTA WRESTLING PROGRAM

Thanks to the help from my head coach and Charild Millard, I finally got in contact with one of the coaches of the Minnesota Wrestling Program. It was Trevor Brandvold. On September 15, 2023, I finally got to meet Trevor in-person, attend practice, and meet all the coaches and wrestlers who were there at the time. Gable Steveson, the GOLD MEDALIST happened to be there and I was so amazed to meet him in-person at my first practice. I couldn't be more excited than ever. I gotta say, being a Team Manager for the Minnesota Wrestling Program is one of the best decisions I have made in my colleg life not just because I can be teammates with my Askren Wrestling Academy friends (Ethan Riddle, Charlie Millard, Wyatt DuChateau, and Silas Dailey), but also because I have connections with famous people like Gable Steveson, Michael Blockus, Patrick McKee, Andrew Sparks, Vance Vombaur, Max Mcenelly, etc.
